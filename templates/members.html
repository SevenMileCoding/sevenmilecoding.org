Board

Seven Mile Coding is made up of student volunteers who study computer science at the University of Michigan. Our mentors bring their experience and knowledge directly to the students in the Detroit community. Seven Mile Coding is a part of Seven Mile Music, a nonprofit organization that works to provide accessible education to youth in Detroit.

Maverick Cook - Chair

Maverick is a senior in computer science at the University of Michigan. After being introduced to coding in college, he wished he learned how to code earlier, and has been working to make coding more accessible ever since.

Tara Safavi - Director of Teaching

Tara is a PhD student in computer science at the University of Michigan. During her time at college, Tara has dedicated time to teaching and mentoring fellow students in multiple computer science classes.

Ritam Mehta - Director of Curriculum

A senior in computer science at the University of Michigan, Ritam has spent his summers developing computer science curriculum for the City of New York.
